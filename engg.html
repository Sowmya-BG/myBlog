Shridevi Institute of Engineering & Technology (SIET), Tumakuru, Karnataka is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, Karnataka, and is approved by AICTE New Delhi. Shridevi Institute of Engineering & Technology is a brainchild of Honorable Dr. M. R. Hulinaykar, (Chairman and Managing Trustee, SCT, Tumakuru). He is the President of Sri Shridevi Charitable Trust (R.), which is running 13 Institutions under various streams of education. His love for education and care for his native district manifested itself in the form of this college.

SIET was established in the year 2002 with the sole aim to impart quality Technical Education. It is approved by AICTE & affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi Karnataka state and presently offers undergraduate & postgraduate Programmes with annual intake of 540 & 150 respectively.

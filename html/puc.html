Sree Siddaganga PU College for Women, B.H. Road, Tumkur, is run by Sree Siddaganga Education Society, Sree Siddaganga Math, Tumkur. The Society is headed by His Holiness Sree Sree Shivakumara Mahaswamigalu, the ‘Abhinava Basavanna’. SSES took it's birth in 1963.

The society runs various kinds of educational institutions ranging from Samskruta Patashalas to Technical Colleges. They are 128 in number.The College offers education in 3 streams viz., Arts, Science and Commerce.

There are 17 departments viz., Kannada, English, Hindi, Urdu, Sanskrit, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Electronics, Computer Science, Commerce, History, Economics, Political Science, Sociology and Education. Each department has adequate faculty who are well experienced and have contemporary subject knowledge. Sree Siddaganga PU College for Women came into being in 2001-02, when the original college, was bifurcated into PU and Degree Colleges, as per government order.
